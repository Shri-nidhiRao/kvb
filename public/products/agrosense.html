The Brain of Your Farm

AgroSense is an advanced precision agriculture platform designed to convert conventional farming into a data-driven, automated, and intelligent system. The platform continuously acquires and processes multi-layer soil data, environmental conditions, and crop requirements, enabling automated irrigation, intelligent fertigation, and predictive crop protection. It functions as a closed-loop agricultural intelligence system, where real-time data directly drives control actions, improving efficiency, reducing resource wastage, and enhancing crop productivity.

System Architecture

A closed-loop feedback system designed for continuous agricultural optimization.

1. Field Layer

Rugged, weather-resistant sensory nodes capture real-time multi-depth soil telemetry and environmental data across your farm.

2. Control Layer

Embedded microcontrollers perform depth-based comparative analysis to generate instant control signals for valves and pumps.

3. Cloud Layer

Secure cloud infrastructure providing data storage, machine learning analytics, and an intuitive remote visualization dashboard.
