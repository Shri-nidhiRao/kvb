Food Dehydrator System

Clean, efficient, and highly sustainable professional solutions designed by KVB Green Energies to meet the demands of modern industry.

Eco-Friendly High Efficiency

Controlled Drying for Premium Quality

The Food Dehydrator System is a high‑efficiency drying solution designed to remove moisture under controlled temperature, airflow, and humidity. It ensures uniform drying, enhanced product quality, extended shelf life, and hygienic processing – making it ideal for commercial applications in agro‑processing, food industries, and export‑oriented production. Transform raw produce into value‑added, market‑ready products with consistent quality.

Why Choose This Dehydrator

Superior Drying Performance

  • 40–90°C adjustable temperature range
  • Forced convection for uniform drying
  • Moisture reduction up to 80–95%
  • 4–24 hours drying time (product dependent)

Robust Construction

  • SS 304 food‑grade contact surfaces
  • PUF / glass wool insulation
  • Stainless steel perforated trays
  • Tray capacities from 10 to 200 trays

Smart Features (Optional)

  • Programmable drying cycles
  • Digital PID temperature & humidity control
  • IoT remote monitoring & data logging
  • Automatic safety shutdown

Economic & Environmental Benefits

  • Faster drying than open sun
  • Retains color, taste, and nutrients
  • Reduces post‑harvest losses
  • Payback period: 1–3 years
