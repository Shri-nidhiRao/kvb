⚡ Scheffler Fixed‑Focus Technology

The Direct Cooking uses a patented lateral‑moving parabolic geometry to reflect sunlight onto a stationary receiver located inside the kitchen. Unlike conventional cookers, the cooking position is fixed, safe, and indoor. The dish automatically tracks the sun while the focal point remains static — enabling continuous, high‑temperature cooking without operator exposure to concentrated rays.
