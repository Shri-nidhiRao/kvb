⚡ Parabolic Concentrator Technology

The Solar Parabolic Cooker harnesses the power of a precisely curved parabolic reflector to concentrate sunlight onto a small focal point, instantly generating intense heat (up to 350°C). This concentrated energy is transferred directly to a cooking vessel, enabling boiling, frying, roasting, and steaming without any fuel. The system features a simple manual tracking mechanism to follow the sun, ensuring peak performance throughout the day. Ideal for clean, sustainable cooking in both rural and urban settings.
