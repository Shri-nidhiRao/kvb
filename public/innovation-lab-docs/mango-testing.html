See Inside Every Mango – Without Cutting

Today, mango grading is mostly manual and based on external color and size – leading to inconsistent quality and lost value. Our upcoming Mango Quality Testing & Segregation System solves this by using advanced intelligent analysis to understand ripeness, internal defects, and true quality. Using thermal patterns and structured logic, it detects spoilage and maturity without cutting the fruit, ensuring every mango is sorted accurately for its real market value.
