How It Works – Simple & Accurate

Step 1: Feeding & Pre‑Cleaning

  • Raw pepper loaded onto conveyor
  • Removes dust, stones, and very light particles

Step 2: Smart Inspection

  • Each seed scanned in real‑time
  • Identifies pepper seeds vs. adulterants (papaya seeds, etc.)
  • Detects defective or low‑quality berries

Step 3: Intelligent Decision

  • Classifies into: Export Premium, Standard Grade, Light/Low‑Quality, Adulterated/Reject
  • Based on shape, texture, and pattern analysis

Step 4: Automatic Segregation

  • System separates pepper into categories
  • No manual sorting – high speed, high accuracy

Smart Purity & Quality Assurance

In today’s market, black pepper is often mixed with papaya seeds, stones, or light berries – materials that look similar to the naked eye. Manual sorting cannot reliably detect such adulteration, leading to export rejections and loss of trust. Our upcoming Pepper Segregation & Adulteration Detection System uses intelligent automated inspection to analyze each seed’s shape, texture, and pattern differences in real‑time, ensuring that only pure, high‑quality pepper reaches the market.
