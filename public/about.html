Our Agritech Intelligence Stack

Smart Irrigation

Closed‑loop autonomous water optimization: real‑time soil moisture, weather integration, automated control. 30–50% water savings.

Soil Nutrient Intelligence

NPK modeling, EC tracking, salinity mapping, deficiency prediction, data‑driven fertigation.

Land Regeneration Index

Quantifiable soil health: organic carbon, moisture retention, nutrient density, biological activity, long‑term regeneration score.

Soil Microbiome Intelligence

Proxy‑based biological monitoring using moisture, carbon, nutrient balance, crop stress signals.

Predictive Crop Immunity

Disease prevention before infection: climate modeling + plant stress signals for early outbreak prediction.

Climate Ledger

Measurable regenerative performance: carbon impact, water efficiency, soil carbon trends, energy optimization, regenerative score benchmarking.
