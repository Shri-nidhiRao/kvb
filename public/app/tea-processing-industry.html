Reliable & efficient system

System Process

  • Solar mirrors capture and concentrate sunlight
  • Heat is focused onto absorber tubes
  • Heat transfer fluid absorbs thermal energy
  • Steam or hot air is generated
  • Heat is supplied to tea processing operations
  • Boiler backup ensures continuous operation

Integration Options

  • Boiler Feedwater Preheating
  • Direct Steam Supply
  • Hot Air Generation for Drying
  • Hybrid Solar + Boiler Mode
  • Thermal Storage (optional)

Industrial Performance
