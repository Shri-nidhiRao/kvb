The Challenge

Farmers face significant losses due to post-harvest spoilage, lack of storage, and dependence on weather for open-sun drying, leading to poor quality and lower market prices.

KVB Solution

We offer hygienic Solar Tunnel Dryers and Smart Greenhouse solutions that protect produce, ensure uniform drying, and integrate IoT sensors for precise climate control.

Agri-Tech Innovations

Solar Tunnel Dryers

UV‑stabilized polycarbonate structures for hygienic drying of fruits, vegetables, spices, and fish.

  • Capacity: 50kg to 5 Tons
  • Temp Rise: 15°C – 30°C above ambient
  • Protection: Dust, rain, and insect proof

Smart Greenhouses

Climate‑controlled environments for high‑value crops with automated irrigation and fertigation.

  • Control: Temp, Humidity, CO₂
  • Automation: Mobile app monitoring
  • Yield: 3‑4x higher productivity
