Solar Steam Cooking

Harnessing the power of concentrated solar energy for large‑scale cooking and industrial heating, replacing fossil fuels with clean, renewable thermal energy.

Parabolic Solar Cooker

Our parabolic solar cookers use a precision-engineered reflective dish to concentrate sunlight onto a focal point, generating temperatures high enough for large-scale community and institutional cooking. Ideal for hostels, temples, and army kitchens — completely eliminating LPG and firewood dependency.

Learn More

Solar Scheffler Dish

The Scheffler dish is a fixed-focus solar concentrator that tracks the sun automatically, delivering consistent high-temperature heat throughout the day. Perfect for steam generation and large-scale cooking with zero fuel costs and minimal maintenance.

Learn More

Direct Cooking Systems

Our direct solar cooking systems deliver focused heat directly to cooking vessels, eliminating the need for intermediate heat transfer fluids. Ideal for institutional kitchens where simplicity, efficiency, and reliability are paramount.

Learn More

Solar Parabolic Trough

Parabolic trough collectors concentrate solar radiation onto a receiver tube running along the focal line of the trough. They generate medium to high temperature heat for industrial process heating, steam generation, and thermal energy applications across diverse industries.
