Hybrid solar tunnel dryers utilize solar energy in combination with an auxiliary biomass or electric heating system to deliver uninterrupted and efficient drying performance, even during unfavorable weather conditions. Smart Fish Dryer

Hybrid solar fish dryers are engineered to provide a hygienic and controlled drying solution for fish and seafood products. Combining solar energy with a backup heating system, they ensure reliable and efficient drying performance even under varying weather conditions. With optimized airflow and ventilation, these dryers minimize contamination, reduce spoilage, and deliver uniform drying for consistently high-quality output.
