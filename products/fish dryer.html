Smart Fish Dryer

🐟 Smart Solar‑Assisted Seafood Drying

The Smart Fish Dryer is a next‑generation, hygienic drying system engineered specifically for fish and seafood processing. It integrates solar thermal energy, biomass heating, and intelligent control systems to deliver consistent, high‑quality drying with 24×7 operational capability. Designed for modern processing units, the system ensures odor‑controlled operation, uniform moisture removal, and export‑grade product quality – ideal for fisheries, coastal industries, and value‑added seafood businesses.

Core Capabilities

Drying Performance

  • Temperature: 45–80°C (solar), up to 100°C (assisted)
  • Moisture reduction: 70–90%
  • Drying time: 6–24 hours (fish type dependent)
  • Uniform texture, improved color, export‑grade finish

Smart Automation

  • Digital PID temperature control
  • Humidity sensors for precision drying
  • Programmable batch cycles
  • IoT‑enabled remote monitoring (optional)
  • Data logging for traceability & HACCP

Smart Heating Integration

  • Solar thermal as primary heat
  • Biomass backup (briquettes, coconut shell, wood pellets)
  • Indirect heating – zero smoke contamination
  • Fully synchronized with smart control

Sustainability & Profitability

  • Reduces drying time by 50–70%
  • Eliminates open‑sun contamination
  • Improves product value for export
  • Payback: 1–2 years | CO₂ reduction: 30–200 t/year
