System Overview

The 16 m² Solar Scheffler Dish is a precision‑engineered concentrated solar cooking solution based on the fixed‑focus principle. It reflects sunlight onto a stationary indoor receiver, enabling seamless, fuel‑free cooking for temples, residential schools, industrial canteens, and mid‑day meal programs. With an optical efficiency of 65–75% and a robust galvanized steel structure, it delivers reliable thermal output with minimal maintenance.

Key Specifications

Aperture Area
