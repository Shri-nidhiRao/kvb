Solar Scheffler Dish(Solar Steam Cooking)

⚡ Scheffler Fixed‑Focus Technology

The 16 m² Solar Scheffler Dish uses a patented lateral‑moving parabolic geometry to reflect sunlight onto a stationary receiver located inside the kitchen. Unlike conventional cookers, the cooking position is fixed, safe, and indoor. The dish automatically tracks the sun while the focal point remains static — enabling continuous, high‑temperature cooking without operator exposure to concentrated rays.
