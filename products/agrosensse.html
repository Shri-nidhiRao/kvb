AgroSense – Smart Farming & Precision Agriculture Platform

AI-Driven Soil Intelligence, Automated Irrigation & Smart Crop Protection

🔧System Overview

AgroSense is an advanced precision agriculture platform designed to convert conventional farming into a data-driven, automated, and intelligent system.

The platform continuously acquires and processes multi-layer soil data, environmental conditions, and crop requirements, enabling automated irrigation, intelligent fertigation, and predictive crop protection.

It functions as a closed-loop agricultural intelligence system, where real-time data directly drives control actions, improving efficiency, reducing resource wastage, and enhancing crop productivity.

🌾Multi-Layer Soil Intelligence System

AgroSense performs depth-based soil analysis, focusing on the root-zone, where nutrient uptake is critical.

Parameters Monitored:

Nitrogen (N)
Phosphorus (P)
Potassium (K)
Soil Moisture
Soil Temperature
Soil pH
Electrical Conductivity

👉 Key Innovation: Depth-gradient comparative analysis

The system evaluates variations across soil layers, enabling:

  • Root-zone-specific nutrient delivery
  • Accurate irrigation decisions
  • Reduction of nutrient loss and leaching
