Box Type Solar Dryer

Greenhouse‑Effect Drying Technology

The Box Type Solar Dryer operates on the natural greenhouse principle: solar radiation passes through a transparent glazing, is absorbed inside an insulated chamber, and heats the air. Natural convection (or optional forced circulation) removes moisture from the produce, resulting in uniform, contamination‑free drying. This technology eliminates open‑sun drying losses, reduces drying time by 30–50%, and preserves nutritional quality, colour, and market value.
